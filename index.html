R Rstudio

Understanding bike cycling frequency and trends for members vs casual cyclists.

Both groups ride much more during afternoons. Members cycle more during the weekdays than the weekends when compared with casual cyclists. Casual cyclists cycle more on the weekends during early afternoon. Although members on average have cycle more in terms of number of trips, casual cyclists cycle for a longer duration per ride, regardless of the type of bike (classic, docked, or electric).

Both groups cycle for about the same distance and prefer classic or docked bike way more than electric bikes. This suggests that both groups don't use bikes solely for as a means of transportation, but for other purposes such as health benefits.
